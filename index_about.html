About me

Developer, designer,
problem solver.

I care deeply about craft — both in code and in the experiences people use every day.

Hey, I'm Kennedy Kamau Njuguna — a full-stack developer and UI/UX designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I studied Computer Science at Dedan Kimathi University and have spent the last three years building products that people actually enjoy using.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering and design. I believe the best digital products are ones where you can't tell the developer and designer were different people — the seams don't show. I'm obsessive about performance, accessibility, and the small details that make an interface feel alive.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm hiking in the Ngong Hills, shooting street photography around Nairobi, or mentoring students through the Africa Coding Network.
