Four button style variants: primary, secondary, danger, and disabled — each with hover and active states demonstrated.

Primary

Solid background · high emphasis

Background shifts two stops darker on hover. Scale on active.

Secondary

Outline · medium emphasis

Transparent fill; border and fill tighten on hover.

Danger

Destructive actions · high and medium emphasis

Filled red for primary destructive; ghost red for secondary destructive.

Disabled

Not interactive · muted, no hover

Low contrast, reduced opacity, cursor: not-allowed. No hover state.
